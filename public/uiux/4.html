üß† What is an Empathy Map?

An Empathy Map helps you understand your user's experience in four quadrants:

  1. Says ‚Äî What the user says out loud
  2. Thinks ‚Äî What they really think
  3. Does ‚Äî Actions they take
  4. Feels ‚Äî Emotional reactions

üéØ Why Use It?

It deepens empathy and reveals hidden needs beyond what users explicitly state.

üßæ Example: (Grocery App User - Amina)

Says
  • "I don't have time"
  • "This is too slow"
Thinks
  • "I hope I didn't forget milk"
  • "I hate doing this every week"
Does
  • Repeats shopping lists
  • Deletes pop-ups frequently
Feels
  • Annoyed, rushed, frustrated
  • Impatient

üß© How to Create Your Own Personas & Maps

Step 1: Collect User Data

  • Interviews (5-10 users is enough)
  • Surveys
  • App reviews or online feedback

Step 2: Group Similar Behaviors

  • Look for patterns in goals, needs, or frustrations
  • Segment by user type if needed (e.g., first-time users, frequent buyers)

Step 3: Create 2-3 Personas

  • Focus on your core audience
  • Keep them simple, clear, and actionable

Step 4: Build an Empathy Map for Each

  • Use a 2x2 grid
  • Populate from real feedback or assumptions based on interviews
